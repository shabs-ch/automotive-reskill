# User Feedback Round 1 — Day 14

**Date**: Week 3, Day 14
**Profile**: Automotive engineer, ~15 years experience
**Duration**: 30 minutes screen share

## Issues identified

1. **Score labels unclear**: Job matching score (55-70%) and 
   Role family fit (82%) not self-explanatory. User confused 
   about what each means.

2. **Match rationale vague/truncated**: "Why this matches" shows 
   raw job ad snippet — not useful. User expects an explanation 
   of WHY their skills match this role.

3. **Gap analysis delay**: System takes time before "Analyse Gap" 
   button becomes responsive — user unsure if system is working.

4. **Section labels unclear**: "Transfers With Bridging" and 
   "You Need To Learn" not self-explanatory without context.
   "Recommended First Steps" not clearly linked to the sections above.

5. **Gap analysis dropdown shows all 4 families**: Should only 
   show role families from matched results — showing all 4 
   is confusing when only 1-2 were recommended.

6. **Score range 55-70%**: Feels low to users even when these 
   are genuine matches. Scale not intuitive.

## What worked well
- Overall flow made sense once explained
- Gap analysis output quality was strong
- Role family fit score concept resonated once explained

## Fix priority
High: #2, #4, #5
Medium: #1, #6, #3
