##Opening — get them talking:

Walk me through what your last 6 months have looked like since the transition started.
What does a typical week look like for you right now?

##Pain discovery:

What's been the hardest part of figuring out where to go next?
What did you search for online that gave you useless results?
Where did you feel most lost or stuck?

##Current behaviour — what are they doing TODAY:

What retraining programs did you look at and why did you reject them?
Have you used any tools or services to help with the transition? What worked, what didn't?
How are you currently figuring out which roles to target?

##Specific to your product hypothesis:

When you look at a job ad for an AI role, what's your reaction?
Do you feel your automotive experience is valued or invisible in AI job applications?
Have you tried rewriting your CV for AI roles? What was that like?

##Bildungsgutschein — this is gold:

Are you aware of the Bildungsgutschein? Have you tried to use it?
If yes — walk me through that process. What was confusing?
If no — why not?

##Forward looking:

What would have to be true for a tool to actually help you?
What's the one thing you wish existed that doesn't?

##Closing:

Is there anything I didn't ask that you think I should know?
Do you know anyone else going through this I should talk to?
